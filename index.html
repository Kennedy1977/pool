Single-Player 8-Ball

Pool Night

A camera-angled browser table with rolling balls, drag-to-shoot control, WPA-style call-shot 8-ball rules, and a CPU opponent that plays to the same rule set.

Drag empty space to orbit the table, right-drag or hold Shift to pan, and use the wheel or trackpad scroll to zoom. Click the cue ball to enter shot mode, pick your target, then drag back from the cue ball to set power and shoot.
