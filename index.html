Pool Night

A camera-angled browser table with rolling balls, assisted shot selection, cue-ball spin and power controls, WPA-style call-shot 8-ball rules, and a CPU opponent that plays to the same rule set.

Drag empty space to orbit the table, right-drag or hold Shift to pan, and use the wheel or trackpad scroll to zoom. Click the cue ball to enter shot mode, or let the game preload the next legal shot for you. The camera swings behind the cue ball for each suggestion, and you can still fine-tune the angle, spin, and vertical power bar before clicking the cue ball again to shoot.
